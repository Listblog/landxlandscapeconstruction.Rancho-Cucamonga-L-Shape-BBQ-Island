Why L-Shape BBQ Islands Are the Most Popular Design

  • The L-shaped layout offers flexibility, as we saw with a client in Rancho Cucamonga who wanter their island to be a centerpiece in their 25x25 space.
  • It provides plenty of room for cooking, prepping, and serving while leaving space for entertaining guests.
  • Perfect for accommodating additional features like the umbrella we recommended to give them some shade without losing the sunlight they love.

Top Accessories for Your Rancho Cucamonga L-Shape BBQ Island

Let's take inspiration from the island we recently built:

  • Grill and Griddle: The client loved grilling and wanted plenty of space to cook and serve food.
  • Mini Fridge: Keeping drinks cool was a priority, so we added a compact fridge.
  • Sink: Cleaning up on the spot is much easier with a built-in sink.
  • Concrete Countertops: The client wanted durable, stylish countertops, so we went with custom-poured concrete.
  • Overhang Seating: To maximize space without making the island bigger, we suggested a 1-foot overhang for bar seating. They loved the idea and so did their guests!
  • Umbrella: Instead of a bulky patio cover, we added an umbrella for a sleek touch of shade.
